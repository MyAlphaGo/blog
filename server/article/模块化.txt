<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">引入：当一个模块的内部需要用到另外一个模块暴露的方法或对象，这时就需要将另外一个模块引入。</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">依赖：A模块的内部实现需要用到B模块暴露的方法或对象，就称之为：A依赖于B</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;">&nbsp;</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">将一个复杂的程序依据一定的规则(规范)封装成几个块(文件), 并进行组合在一起</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">块的内部数据与实现是私有的, 只是向外部暴露一些接口(方法)与外部其它模块通信</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;">&nbsp;</p>

<ul style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 16px 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; list-style: none; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;">
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">避免命名冲突(减少命名空间污染)</span></span></li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">更好的分离, 按需加载</span></span></li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">更高复用性</span></span></li>
<li style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; color: #333333;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">高可维护性</span></span></span></li>
</ul>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff; text-indent: 2em;" align="left"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">问题</span><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">：</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff; text-indent: 2em;" align="left"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">请求过多:</span>&nbsp;<span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">首先我们要依赖多个模块，那样就会发送多个请求，导致请求过多</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff; text-indent: 2em;" align="left"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">依赖模糊：我们不知道他们的具体依赖关系是什么，也就是说很容易因为不了解他们之间的依赖关系导致加载先后顺序出错。</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff; text-indent: 2em;" align="left"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">难以维护：以上两种原因就导致了很难维护，很可能出现牵一发而动全身的情况导致项目出现严重的问题。</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff; text-indent: 2em;" align="left"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: inherit; line-height: 1.75; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">模块化固然有多个好处，然而一个页面需要引入多个js文件，就会出现以上这些问题。而这些问题可以通过模块化规范来解决，下面介绍开发中最流行的ES6规范。</span></span></p>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline; color: #333333;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-style: inherit; font-variant: inherit; font-weight: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; font-size: 18.72px; line-height: inherit; font-family: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;"><span style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font: inherit; vertical-align: baseline;">4.ES6模块化</span></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff; text-indent: 2em;">ES6模块的设计思想是尽量的静态化，使得编译时就能确认模块的依赖关系。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="box-sizing: border-box; margin: 0px; padding: 0px; border: 0px; font-variant-numeric: inherit; font-variant-east-asian: inherit; font-stretch: inherit; line-height: inherit; font-family: 'Microsoft YaHei'; vertical-align: baseline; min-height: 1em; color: rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.65); background-color: #ffffff;">ES6模块化语法</p>
